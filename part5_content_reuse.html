Part V: Content Reuse

Learn about the importance, methods, and best practices for content reuse in DITA.

Upon completing this module, you will be able to:

  • Explain the critical importance of content reuse in DITA and its business benefits.
  • Identify and differentiate between key DITA attributes for conditional processing (audience, platform, product, otherprops).
  • Describe how conref enables element-level reuse and best practices for its use.
  • Understand when to use copy-to versus conref for content reuse.
  • Illustrate practical examples of content reuse in various real-world scenarios.
